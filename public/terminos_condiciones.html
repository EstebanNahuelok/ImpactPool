01. Fund Separation Rules

ImpactPool maintains a strict non-commingling policy for all funds held in the protocol's liquidity pools.

1.1

All donor contributions are directed to isolated smart contracts specific to the chosen impact vertical (e.g., Clean Water, Education).

1.2

Operational capital for ImpactPool platform maintenance is held in a separate "Treasury Vault" and never sourced from active impact funds.

1.3

Assets remain on-chain in transparent ledger accounts, visible to any stakeholder at any time through the Public Ledger interface.

02. Yield Limitations for Sponsors

ImpactPool uses sustainable yield-generation protocols to ensure fund longevity. However, sponsor participation is subject to "Social Impact First" restrictions.

Capped Yields

Sponsors may only claim up to 2% of the annual yield generated as "Incentive Rewards". The remaining yield is automatically reinvested into the impact objective.

Principal Lock

Contributed principal capital is locked for the duration of the impact cycle. Early withdrawal is subject to a 15% "Stabilization Fee" directed to the beneficiary pool.

03. Multisig Governance

To prevent centralized failures or misappropriation, all significant fund disbursements require M-of-N signatures.

  • check_circle A minimum of 3 out of 5 designated "Impact Guardians" must approve any transaction exceeding 5,000 USD in value.
  • check_circle Guardians consist of independent auditors, community leaders, and elected ImpactPool delegates.
  • check_circle All signature metadata, including timestamp and wallet origin, is permanently recorded on the immutable ledger.

Note: In the event of a security compromise, a temporary 48-hour lock is imposed on all outgoing transactions to allow for a community audit.

04. Beneficiary Data Privacy

While financial flows are public, the personal identity of individual beneficiaries is protected by Zero-Knowledge Proof (ZKP) technology.

Data Category Visibility Status Retention
Transaction Amount PUBLIC Permanent (On-chain)
Beneficiary Real Name ENCRYPTED 90 Days (Off-chain)
Biometric Verification ENCRYPTED Discarded after validation
