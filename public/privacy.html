01. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate the platform and process donations securely.

1.1

Account Information: Email address, name, and encrypted password when you register.

1.2

Wallet Data: Public wallet addresses for blockchain interactions. We never access your private keys.

1.3

Transaction Records: Donation amounts, timestamps, and campaign associations stored on-chain for transparency.

1.4

Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage patterns to improve platform experience. No personal identifiers are tracked.

analytics

02. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for platform operations and never sold to third parties.

Platform Operations

Processing donations, managing accounts, and facilitating communication between donors and organizations.

Transparency Reporting

Generating verifiable impact reports while maintaining beneficiary anonymity through zero-knowledge proofs.

share

03. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Information may be shared only in these limited circumstances:

  • check_circleWith blockchain networks for transaction processing (public wallet addresses only).
  • check_circleWith partner organizations when you explicitly authorize a donation distribution.
  • check_circleWhen required by law or to protect the rights and safety of platform users.

05. Your Rights

You have control over your personal data at all times.

5.1

Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

5.2

Correction: Update or correct any inaccurate information in your profile.

5.3

Deletion: Request account deletion. Note: on-chain transaction records are immutable by design.

5.4

Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format upon request.

For privacy inquiries, contact us at privacy@impactpool.org. This policy may be updated with 30 days prior notice.
