General

What is ImpactPool? expand_more expand_less
ImpactPool is a decentralized donation platform that ensures transparency and accountability. Every contribution is tracked on the Avalanche blockchain, allowing donors to verify exactly how their funds are distributed and used by partner organizations.
How does the 70/30 model work? expand_more expand_less
When you make a donation, 70% goes directly to the beneficiaries through digital vouchers that can be redeemed for essential goods and services. The remaining 30% is held in a blockchain-based vault (DonationVault) to generate sustainable yield that funds ongoing operations and future impact initiatives.
Who can use ImpactPool? expand_more expand_less
Anyone can register as a donor to contribute to campaigns. Non-profit organizations (NGOs) can apply for a partnership account to create campaigns and distribute vouchers to their beneficiaries. All users must complete identity verification.

Donations

What payment methods are supported? expand_more expand_less
ImpactPool supports cryptocurrency donations via MetaMask and Coinbase Wallet (AVAX, USDC on Avalanche), as well as the x402 HTTP payment protocol for on-chain payments. Future updates will integrate traditional payment rails through Fiserv/Clover POS systems.
Can I choose which campaign to support? expand_more expand_less
Yes. When making a donation, you can select a specific campaign from the available campaigns list. Each campaign shows its goal, current progress, and the organization managing it, so you can make an informed decision.
Are donations tax-deductible? expand_more expand_less
Tax deductibility depends on your jurisdiction and the specific organization receiving the funds. ImpactPool provides blockchain-verified receipts that can be used as proof of donation for tax purposes. Consult your local tax advisor for specific guidance.

Digital Vouchers

What are digital vouchers? expand_more expand_less
Digital vouchers are blockchain-verified tokens distributed to beneficiaries by partner organizations. Each voucher has a specific monetary value and can be redeemed for essential goods and services at approved vendors. The 70% direct-impact portion of every donation is converted into these vouchers.
How are vouchers distributed? expand_more expand_less
Partner organizations create campaigns and assign vouchers to verified beneficiaries. Each voucher is recorded on the blockchain along with the beneficiary's anonymous identifier, the assigned value, and a unique redemption code. Organizations manage distribution through their dashboard.

Blockchain & Technology

Why Avalanche blockchain? expand_more expand_less
Avalanche provides fast transaction finality (under 2 seconds), low fees, and high throughput — critical for a donation platform processing many small transactions. Its support for custom subnets (L1s) allows ImpactPool to operate on a dedicated chain optimized for social impact operations.
What is the x402 payment protocol? expand_more expand_less
x402 is an HTTP-native payment protocol that enables on-chain payments as part of standard web requests. It allows ImpactPool to accept donations via API calls with automatic on-chain settlement, making the payment experience seamless for users and developers integrating with our platform.
Do I need a crypto wallet to donate? expand_more expand_less
While crypto wallets (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet) provide the most seamless experience, ImpactPool is designed to support multiple payment methods. You can also sign up with email and password and donate through supported payment channels. Your on-chain activity will be recorded even without a personal wallet.
